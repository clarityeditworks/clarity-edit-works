What we include in a free sample

Request a complimentary sample edit (up to 1,000 words) so you can see our approach — whether you choose Developmental, Line, or Copy editing. We provide clear, actionable feedback while preserving your voice. Submit the request form and we’ll email your sample back with notes.

Developmental Sample

Focused on big-picture story elements: structure, pacing, character arcs, and thematic clarity. The sample includes an editorial letter with prioritized recommendations.

Line Editing Sample

Sentence-level polish: rhythm, clarity, and word choice. Ideal for tightening prose while maintaining voice and emotional cadence.

Copy Editing Sample

Technical accuracy: grammar, punctuation, consistency, and formatting. Perfect for manuscripts preparing for submission or publication.

How the free sample works

  1. Choose the type of editing you want (Developmental, Line, or Copy).
  2. Attach or paste up to 1,000 words in the request form — include context and specific concerns if any.
  3. We review and return a sample with comments and a short editorial summary via email.

If you prefer, you can also email your request directly to clarityeditworks@gmail.com.
